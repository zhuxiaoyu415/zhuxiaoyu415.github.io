My world

This is a new world of mine. Hope I will grow with it, and you will grow with me.

If I were a boy, I think I would marry Rachel

Soon will be one year since I have moved in with Rachel. After more than one year, we started to have more and more in common.She is such a great girl who deserves a best man, since I am leaving her now, I think it’s about time for her man to go and get her.

Days will never go back again, so these flashes in Bangkok, Kio Simou, Angkor Wat,Jeju Island, as well as places all around the corner of Beijing. She has been a very good friend, or the other side of me.

However, friends are bond to share one certain journey then fly away, to seek our own freedom.

I guess what I am saying is no matter where I go, this girl I will never forget. I only hope everything good with her, everything is perfect.

God bless her!
